## About art and sacredness

**Question 1** – What is art?  
*Response categories:* (a) objective and (b) subjective.

**Question 2** – What makes it sacred?  
*Response categories:* the Church's definition according to the Catechism (a), personal hermeneutics/doxa (b), or even a parallel teaching reconciled with Catholic teaching (c).

**Question 3** – What makes art beautiful?  
*Response categories:* technical, objective, and universal response (a), or personal, subjective, and individual response (b).
